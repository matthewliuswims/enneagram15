Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

Enneagram 15 is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at www.enneagram15.com.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Our personality quiz runs entirely in your browser. Your answers are never sent to a server and are discarded when you close or refresh the page.

Cookies and Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics trackers, or any third-party tracking scripts. We do not use advertising networks or retargeting services.

Third-Party Services

Our site loads fonts from Google Fonts. Google may collect basic usage data (such as your IP address) when serving font files. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share any user data with third parties because we do not collect any user data.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please reach out to us through our website.
